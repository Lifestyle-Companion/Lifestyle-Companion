ALPHA 1.2 DECISION LOG

- The Companion must teach the application rather than rely on a separate manual.
- Onboarding must support a voice-guided usability test and a typing-only usability test.
- All Rooms remain visible on Home whether configured, unconfigured or coming soon.
- Room-specific personal information is collected only when that Room is activated.
- Settings is a permanent Home Circle Room.
- Users can store multiple labelled addresses and phone numbers.
- Users can teach name pronunciation through a spoken sample and phonetic spelling.
- Water support begins as optional Companion check-ins, not a user-chosen daily target.
- Every main page has a Back control.
- Alpha 1.2 intentionally starts with a clean data store.
- Coming Soon Room taps and explicit interest are counted locally as a prototype of future product analytics.
