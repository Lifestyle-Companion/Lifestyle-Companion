Lifestyle Companion — Founder Decision Log

1. Hymie is a companion first, not merely a calculator.
2. Capture First. Organise Later.
3. Food & Nutrition Plan covers meals, snacks, drinks, hydration and nutrition balance.
4. Exercise & Activity remains a separate Room but links automatically to Food & Nutrition.
5. Weight records long-term progress and links to food and activity.
6. The app should prevent avoidable user mistakes, including carrying quantities forward.
7. Daily nutrition balance should show energy, protein, carbohydrate, fat, fibre and water.
8. Food preferences are collected early and used later for personalised suggestions.
9. “Won’t eat” is a firm exclusion; allergies and intolerances are safety rules.
10. Feedback should remain positive, calm and non-judgemental.
