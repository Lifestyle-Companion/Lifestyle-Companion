Lifestyle Companion — Founder Decision Log
Updated for Version 0.3.1

1. Hymie assumes a food is acceptable unless the user records a preference or restriction.
2. Food preferences are Favourite, Context-dependent/tolerated, Will not eat, and Safety restriction.
3. Allergies and intolerances are structured safety rules, not ordinary dislikes.
4. Context matters: a food may be acceptable within a recipe but not served alone.
5. Users must be able to update food preferences naturally during normal use.
6. All user text fields should support spelling recognition and correction.
7. Confirmation messages should disappear automatically rather than require another tap.
8. Important save actions must be visually prominent.
9. Remaining energy should communicate status at a glance using green, yellow and red.
10. Hymie nutrition suggestions must refresh whenever the food plan changes.
11. Users must be able to backfill or correct records for any date.
12. Dates should include the weekday and identify Today where useful.
13. Page help should combine optional first-time guidance with permanent Help controls.
14. People should not have to remember how to use Lifestyle Companion; it should teach itself as they use it.
