KNOWN ISSUES — ALPHA 1.2

1. Profiles are local to each browser/device. There is no cloud account or iPhone-to-iPad synchronisation yet.
2. Browser speech recognition varies by device and browser. Safari may require microphone permission and an internet connection for recognition.
3. The prototype can record a pronunciation sample and store a phonetic spelling, but browser text-to-speech voices cannot truly be trained from that recording. The real app will need a dedicated pronunciation/voice system.
4. Full address autocomplete is not included. Browser autofill is used where available. A production release needs an Australian address lookup provider and privacy review.
5. Recorded pronunciation audio is stored in browser local storage as a data URL. This is suitable only for small-scale testing.
6. Phone formatting is a basic Australian normaliser, not a complete international phone-number validation library.
7. Voice-only setup is assisted rather than fully hands-free. Selection controls still need tapping in this prototype.
8. Food logging, weight graphs, fasting workflows and cloud analytics remain future work.
