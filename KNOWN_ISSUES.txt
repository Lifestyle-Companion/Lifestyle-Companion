KNOWN ISSUES — ALPHA 1.1

- Profiles and analytics remain local to each browser/device. iPhone and iPad do not yet synchronise.
- Available voice names and quality depend on the operating system and browser.
- Safari may require a direct tap before allowing speech playback. The voice selector itself provides that tap.
- Browser speech recognition support varies. Typed commands remain available.
- Only Food & Nutrition has an activation/setup pathway in this release.
- Food logging, food database, weight graphs, fasting workflows, water logging, recipes and daily summaries are planned for Sprint 2.
- Coming-soon interest data is local only and cannot yet be viewed in Founder Mode or combined across testers.
