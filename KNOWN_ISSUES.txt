Known limitations — Alpha 1.3

1. This browser prototype cannot train a text-to-speech voice from an audio recording. The recording is retained for review; phonetic spelling guides spoken pronunciation.
2. Available system voices are controlled by the browser and device. Alpha 1.3 creates pace/pitch styles when only one voice is exposed, but these are variations of that one installed voice.
3. Verified Australian address autocomplete requires an external address provider and is not connected in this offline/static trial. Speech entry, browser autofill and Same as main address are available now.
4. Speech recognition and microphone recording depend on browser permissions, HTTPS hosting and device support.
5. Data remains on the individual browser/device and is not yet cloud-synchronised.
