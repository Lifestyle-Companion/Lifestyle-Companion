HEALTHY EATING — FOUNDER TRIAL ALPHA 0.3

MAJOR UPGRADE

COMPANION CHOICE
- Companion is now optional.
- The app remains fully usable with written guidance when Companion mode is off.
- Companion can be turned on or off later in Settings.
- Spoken guidance has its own on/off control.
- Saved Companion character, name and voice choices are retained when the Companion is turned off.

LANGUAGE AND APPEARANCE
- Added language and regional-format selection at the beginning of setup.
- Added Garden, Coast, Outback and Classic theme preview cards.
- Selecting a theme immediately previews and applies it.

PERSONAL DETAILS
- Added Preferred name.
- Added pronunciation fields and spoken previews for the user and Companion names.
- Replaced the single address field with Country, State/Region, Postcode, Suburb/City and Street address.
- Added formatted address preview.
- Added country-aware postcode checking.
- Added country-aware phone formatting and international storage form.
- Changed Calories display from kcal to Cal.

HEALTH PROFILE AND GOALS
- Weight-loss-rate choices are now Slow & steady, Recommended and Fast.
- Fasting choices are now No fasting plan or Custom fasting days.
- Custom fasting days reveal weekday and energy-target controls.
- Added the user’s selected goal weight as a separate choice from the recommended goal.
- Added optional future staged goals.
- Calculated recommendations display as clean whole numbers.

PROGRESS
- Weight Check-in is now a working room.
- Check-ins are saved in local history.
- Recommendations recalculate after a weight change of at least 1 kg or a changed goal.
- Small fluctuations are recorded without repeatedly changing the plan.
- Manually chosen energy and nutrient targets are retained during automatic health-estimate refreshes.

HOME
- Added a rotating card containing jokes, quotes, tips, encouragement and food facts.
- Each tap advances to the next item.
- The Companion speaks the item when Companion speech is enabled.
- Home automatically changes to a non-Companion presentation when Companion mode is off.

TECHNICAL
- Added Alpha 0.2 local-storage migration support.
- Updated offline cache name and application metadata.
