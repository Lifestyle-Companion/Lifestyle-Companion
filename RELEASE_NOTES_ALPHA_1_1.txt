LIFESTYLE COMPANION — FOUNDER'S TRIAL EDITION
ALPHA 1.1 — FOUNDATION REFINEMENT

WHAT CHANGED

1. New five-step onboarding wizard
   - About You profile: full name, preferred name, date of birth, email, phone and address.
   - Companion setup: character, name, personality, theme and device voice.
   - Room selection: testers choose which Rooms they want to activate now.
   - Conditional Food & Nutrition setup: health questions appear only when Food & Nutrition is selected.
   - Review page before profile creation.

2. Voice preview
   - Selecting a voice immediately plays a sample greeting.
   - Testers can move between voices and hear each one before deciding.

3. Room visibility and activation
   - All Home Circle Rooms remain visible.
   - Active, not-yet-configured and coming-soon Rooms use subtle status badges.
   - Tapping an available but unconfigured Room starts its setup pathway.
   - Tapping a coming-soon Room displays a friendly message and records local interest.

4. Responsive circles
   - Home and Food & Nutrition navigation use square, aspect-ratio-controlled stages.
   - Mobile scaling was revised to prevent oval distortion and cut-off lower buttons.
   - Additional compact rules apply to shorter phone screens.

5. Preferences & Settings
   - Removed the duplicated Companion editor from the bottom of the page.
   - Added a simple Edit My Companion button.
   - Reuses master profile details automatically.
   - Added current weight, height, goal weight, activity level, fasting preference, water target and dietary notes.

6. Voice interaction sheet
   - Previous command is no longer displayed when opened.
   - The text box is blank and ready for a typed command.
   - Testers may speak, type or use a quick command.

7. Visual refinement
   - Home uses a soft gradient and subtle pattern.
   - Food & Nutrition has a warmer kitchen-inspired background.

8. Local analytics foundation
   - Room taps, setup starts and setup completions are recorded locally in browser storage.
   - No information is sent anywhere in this Alpha build.

DATA COMPATIBILITY
Existing Alpha 1 profiles are automatically migrated where possible. Existing names become both full and preferred names until edited. Existing Food & Nutrition preferences are preserved.
