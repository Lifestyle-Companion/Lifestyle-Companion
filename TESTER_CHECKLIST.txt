Alpha 1.3 tester checklist

1. Start a completely new profile.
2. Test pronunciation recording. Confirm the status clearly changes through Get ready, Recording and Saved.
3. Stop the recording yourself and play it back.
4. Enter the phonetic spelling of the user and Companion names. Check voice previews use that pronunciation.
5. Speak a main address using the microphone.
6. Tick Same as main address for the postal address.
7. Compare every available voice/style using Play, then Select one.
8. Use Back and Continue repeatedly and report any freeze or double-page movement.
9. On Review Profile, scroll through every field, use at least one Edit button, return to Review and tick every entry.
10. After creating the profile, open Settings > Edit Profile and confirm all names, dates, phone numbers and addresses appear.
11. Open Food & Nutrition > Preferences and confirm only Food & Nutrition settings appear.
